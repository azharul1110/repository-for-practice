Egg curry recipe

To make egg curry we need some ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 5 eggs
  • Onion
  • Potato
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt

Cooking step:

  1. First boil both egg and potato.
  2. Then wash it with cold water.
  3. Now take a fringpen and some oil.
  4. Then add chop onion, red chili, salt. Mixed all of them.
  5. Now add boiled eggs and potatos and mixed them. Cook it for 2 minutes.
  6. After two minutes, add some boiled water and cook it for 10 minutes.
  7. Then serve it with rice.
